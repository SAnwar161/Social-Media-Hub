# Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

**Effective Date:** March 26, 2026

**Social Media Hub** ("we," "our," or "us") has a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any form of Child Sexual Abuse or Exploitation (CSAE). 

## 1. Zero Tolerance
We strictly prohibit any use of our application to create, access, share, or promote content that exploits or abuses children. 

## 2. Platform Compliance
**Social Media Hub** is a multi-platform aggregator that provides access to third-party social media services (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, and X). 
- We strictly adhere to and uphold the comprehensive CSAE safety standards of these underlying platforms. 
- Users are bound by the terms of service and safety policies of each platform they access through our hub. 

## 3. Reporting and Enforcement
Any user found attempting to use Social Media Hub for illegal activities involving minors will be subject to:
- Permanent ban from our application.
- Reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and other relevant law enforcement agencies.

## 4. Reporting Illegal Content
If you encounter any suspected child exploitation content while using any platform through our hub, we urge you to:
1.  **Report it directly** to the social media platform using their built-in reporting tools.
2.  **Report it to us** at [aianditservicesltd@gmail.com] so we can take immediate action to block access to such accounts or content within our hub.
3.  **Report it to NCMEC** at [https://report.cybertip.org/](https://report.cybertip.org/).

## 5. Continuous Monitoring
We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and will update our policies and safety features to combat any emerging threats to child safety.
