# Privacy Policy for Element: Social Media Hub

**Effective Date:** March 26, 2026

**Element: Social Media Hub** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to providing a secure and private experience. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") handles your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

*   **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account with Element to use the App.
*   **No Data Harvesting:** We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other identifying information.
*   **No Tracking:** We do not track your location or your behavior across different platforms.

## 2. Platform Authentication (WebViews)

The App functions as a "Hub" using **Native WebViews** to display social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, and X).

*   **Your Credentials:** Any login information you enter (passwords, usernames) is handled directly by the respective social media platform in an isolated browser window. **Element never sees, captures, or stores your passwords.**
*   **Persistent Sessions:** Cookies and session data are managed by your device's native browser engine. When you log out of a platform, that session is cleared from your device according to that platform's own privacy rules.

## 3. Permissions

The App requests the following minimal permissions to function:

*   **Internet Access:** Required to load the social media platforms.
*   **Media/Storage:** Required only if you choose to upload images or videos to a platform (e.g., posting a photo to Instagram).
*   **Clipboard:** Used for the "Social Hub" features (like sharing links) only when you explicitly trigger a share action.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App links to third-party social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or data collection of these external services. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, and X individually.

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
